Bill Splitter

Enter the amount of people splitting the bill in the form below. Click Generate Forms and a new form will pop up for each person you are splitting with. If they have more than 3 items, click "Add Item". Afterwards, enter your tax percentage. If you do not know the tax percentage, enter the tax total in the corresponding form. The app will calculate and apply the tax for you. When all of your information is entered, hit the large submit button at the bottom of the page.

If you'd like to just split the bill evenly, click and enter your total with tax in the form that pops up. Or you could just use the calculator on your phone. Why are you even here?
