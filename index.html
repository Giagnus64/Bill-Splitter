Bill Splitter

Enter the amount of people splitting the bill in the form below. If you'd like to itemize the bill, click Generate Forms. A new form will pop up for each person you are splitting with. If they have more than 3 items, click the "Add item" button until you have enough inputs for every item. Afterwards, enter your tax percentage tax section. If you do not know the tax percentage, simply enter the tax total in the corresponsing input. The site will calculate the tax for you, and apply it accordingly. When all of your information is entered, hit the large submit button at the bottom of the page.

Alternatively, if you like to just split the bill evenly, click the Split Evenly button, and enter your total with tax in the form that pops up. Your bill will be split and you will be shown tip information. Or you could just use the calculator on your phone. Why are you even here?
