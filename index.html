Bill Splitter

Enter the amount of people splitting the bill in the form below. A new form will pop up for each person you are splitting with. If they have more than 3 items, click the "Add item" button until you have enough inputs for every item. Afterwards, enter your state for a tax calculation, or if you have a different tip percentage, enter that into the second input in the tax section. Some states have different tax amounts for alcoholic drinks and food, so if you have a unique situation, or just want to split the tax evenly, simply enter the amount in the third input. When all of your information is entered, hit the large submit button at the bottom of the page.
